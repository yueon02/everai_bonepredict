What is ForensRadioAI?

ForensRadioAI is a software that automates the interpretation of skeletal radiographs such as radiograph and CT scans to estimate biological sex, age-at-death, population affinity, stature, trauma and other osteological traits.

Built using advanced machine learning algorithms and trained on validated datasets, the platform is designed to assist forensic anthropologists, radiologists, and researchers in solving complex human identification cases.
